Hi, I'm Mutaz


I am a PhD student in Kahlert School of Computing at the University of Utah, specializing in HCI under the guidance of Vineet Pandey. I develop and explore tools that help people and healthcare professionals interact more effectively. My research looks at how people use digital health technologies in their daily lives and how those tools can better connect personal health efforts with medical institutions. I focus on making health tracking more useful, not just for individuals but also for healthcare providers. By improving the way people share and use health information, my work helps bridge the gap between everyday health practices and professional care.
