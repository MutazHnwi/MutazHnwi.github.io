Hi, I'm Mutaz


I am a PhD student in Kahlert School of Computing at the University of Utah, specializing in HCI under the guidance of Vineet Pandey. My work focuses on digital health, exploring how technology can improve health assessments and enhance the design of tools for healthcare. My research investigates the design space of digital health systems, emphasizing how we can create effective and scalable solutions for real-world applications. By bridging Human-Computer Interaction principles with healthcare needs, I aim to uncover novel approaches to support clinicians and patients through innovative, data-driven insights and user-centered design.
